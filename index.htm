Our Vision for MRV 

Accessible: Anyone on earth can get paid for their regenerative action, no matter where they are, how big or small the action is, or how much money they have.

Equitable: Communities are empowered through appropriate technology, education, and financial incentives to monitor and restore their local ecosystems.

Holistic: People develop meaningful relationships with their place, supported by contextually relevant and cross-referenced ecosystem data collected from a variety of tools.

Distributed: MRV data gets collected by people who care about the communities and places where regenerative activities are happening and is stored & verified on decentralized infrastructure.

Our Roadmap

Late Spring 2022

dMeter was founded by T.R. and Avano from the money they raised from GR13, Shamba, Astral Protocol, and Athena Protocol

Summer 2022

Started our first pilot, a collaboration between Athena Protocol and Ogallala life to regenerate an area in the drought-ridden Texas panhandle region, implementing a FOAM mesh for gathering a variety of IOT enabled data streams

Winter 2022

Designed our first working and governance structures, using structures inspired by sociocracy. Created a dMRV governance token and distributed it to core leaders.

Won a Grant from Filecoin Green to work on decentralized claim resolution infrastructure in a partnership between dMeter, Regen Network, Silvi, Athena, and Eco Labs.

Spring 2023

Silvi + EcoLabs + FarmOS wins Filecoin green hackathon with Open Trees open source framework for storing tree data on co2.storage.

Planned: Finalize our membership structure, strategy, and working roles. Expand our governance to include dMRV community members, sponsors, and innovators.

Create the DeSci Circle to grow dMeter's network of scientists and facilitate the creation of science backed methodologies.
